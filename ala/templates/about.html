PersonaGram's Goal

We’ve created this e-commerce business as a way to connect University of Michigan students, especially after a year of quarantine. By definition, Persona is “the aspect of someone’s character that is presented to or perceived by others.” We’ve taken this definition to correspond with our personality quiz component and incorporated the candy-gram aspect, thus our business name “PersonaGram”!

Behind PersonaGram

PersonaGram was formulated from an entrepreneurship class (Psych 223: Entrepreneurial Creativity) at the University of Michigan by 4 students!
